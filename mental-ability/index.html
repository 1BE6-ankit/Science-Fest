Rules for the Finals

All questions in the Final will be non-passable, and there no negative marking.
Altogether there will be 3 phases in the finals

PHASE 1
  • Find the hidden meaning: the participants must find a hidden meaning in the given logo. 10 points for each correct answer.
PHASE 2
  • JUMBLED UP: six items will be shown and after few seconds, the six items will be jumbled up where one item will be replaced by another. Naming the lost item will grant 10 points and naming the new item will grant 10 points. Altogether maximum of 20 points can be scored.
PHASE 3
  • Zoom In/ Zoom out: a zoomed-in part of an object will be shown and the participant must name the object. Correct answer will grant 10 points.
  • A surprise BUZZER ROUND will take place in case of tie-breaker

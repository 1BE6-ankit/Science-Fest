Welcome to BNKS Maths Quiz 2016

Rules for the Qualifiers

  • 8 questions will be asked in this round.
  • 10 points will be awarded for the right answer. There will be no negative markings for incorrent or no answer
  • The questions will not be passed.
  • The team gets 45 seconds after the question has been asked.
  • In case of a tie, the tied teams will face questions on the buzzer for the tie breaker. Still if no winner is decided the team with the most marks from the written round will qualify.

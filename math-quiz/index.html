Welcome to BNKS MathsQuiz Finals 2016

Rules for the Finals

  • 8 questions will be asked on the buzzer.
  • The 1st team to press the buzzer will get the chance to answer. 10 points will be awarded for a right answer. 10 points will be deducted in case of a wrong answer or if no answer is given.
  • The questions will not be passed.
  • The teams will have to press the buzzer within 45 seconds after the question has been asked.
  • The teams who are given the chance to answer have to give the answer within 8 seconds after the quiz master has asked them to answer.
  • The teams will not respond until the quizmaster has asked them to answer.

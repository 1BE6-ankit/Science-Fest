Welcome to BNKS ScienceQuiz Finals 2016

Rules for the Finals

The final consists of two rounds, a General Round and a Buzzer Round.
Rules of General Round
  • A total of 8 questions will be asked, starting from the anticlockwise direction and, followed by clockwise direction. Thus each team will get 2 questions.
  • A right answer is awarded with 10 points.
  • No points are deducted in case of a wrong answer or no answer is given.
  • The teams will get 30 seconds to answer the question.
  • The questions will not be passed.
Rules of Buzzer Round
  • 8 questions will be asked on the buzzer.
  • The 1st team to press the buzzer will get the chance to answer. 10 points will be awarded for a right answer. 10 points will be deducted in case of a wrong answer or if no answer is given.
  • The questions will not be passed.
  • The teams will have to press the buzzer within 30 seconds after the question has been asked.
  • The teams who are given the chance to answer have to give the answer within 8 seconds after the quiz master has asked them to answer.
  • The teams will not respond until the quizmaster has asked them to answer.
  • In case of a tie, the tied teams will face questions on the buzzer for the tie breaker.
